WELCOME TO THE INTROBIZ EXPO 2018

Returning to Cardiff for its seventh consecutive year, the Introbiz Business Exhibition has grown year-on-year, now existing as Wales’ largest business expo, headlined this year by Lord Sugar and Grant Cardone. Attracting over 5,000 visitors and 200+ exhibitors, The Apprentice winners and world class keynote speakers from a range of industries and business sectors. Our UK Business Expo brings global and local brands under one roof, having been responsible for some of the most flourishing business relationships in the country. Book your FREE Tickets here!

 

Kickstart the Introbiz Expo 2018 the right way at Wales’ largest networking breakfast held at the Motorpoint Arena Cardiff on Thursday, 22nd of November from 7:00am. This event will be opened by WRU Group CEO Martyn Phillips & BBC Scrum V broadcaster Sean Holley for a live Q&A. Hear from two of the most influential men in Welsh rugby just days before Wales face South Africa in the Autumn Internationals.You’ll be networking with our keynote speakers, celebrity guests, sponsors and exhibitors. Secure many useful business connections and showcase your brand to over four hundred people from the business community before the event is officially open to the general public at 9am. Find out more here!
